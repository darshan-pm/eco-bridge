🌱 Eco-Bridge

Bridging excess to need — instantly, intelligently, and impactfully.

Eco-Bridge is an AI-powered donation platform that transforms the way unused items are shared. Instead of complex forms or manual listings, users simply snap a photo, and AI takes care of everything — from identifying the item to making it instantly available for someone in need.

Built to reduce waste, increase donations, and create a real-time bridge between donors and receivers.

🚀 The Problem

Every day, usable items are wasted because:

Donation processes are slow and inconvenient
People don’t know where or how to donate
NGOs and students struggle to find resources quickly

Eco-Bridge solves this by making donation as simple as taking a photo.

💡 The Solution

📸 Snap → 🤖 AI Understands → ⚡ Instant Listing → 🤝 Immediate Claim

No forms. No delays. No friction.

✨ Key Features
📸 AI-Powered Snap & Donate (Core Engine)
Take a photo of any item
Google Gemini 1.5 Flash identifies:
Item name
Quantity
Condition check (basic safety/quality detection)
Auto-generates donation listing instantly
👥 Role-Based Access System

Eco-Bridge has two clear user paths:

Donor
Upload items via camera
Instantly list donations
Receiver (NGOs / Students)
Browse available items
Claim needed resources instantly

Authentication is handled using lightweight localStorage for fast setup.

🔄 Live Donation Marketplace
Real-time item listing updates
Instant visibility across users
Clean grid-based marketplace UI
One-click Claim Item system
🌍 Impact Dashboard

Eco-Bridge tracks meaningful outcomes:

🌱 CO₂ saved through reuse
⭐ Social Impact Score for donors
📊 Total donations contributed
🔥 Live activity tracking

Making impact visible and motivating continued contribution.
